Top Stories and Tech

The Otto: by Bannana Bros

One part grinder, one part rolling machine, this clever number by Banana Bros is taking the cannabis tech world by storm. The OTTO contains an innovative design that can roll up to 30 joints in one charge and is both odor-proof and spill-proof. Perfect for arthritic hands or a toke on the go, this gadget will be a permanent fixture in your cannabis repertoire. Keep an eye out for a Leafly employee vs. OTTO joint-off!
